What are Twin Fields?

Twin fields let Salesforce CPQ copy field values between the Quote Line and its parent Opportunity Product (Line Item). CPQ looks for matching API field names across SBQQ__QuoteLine__c and OpportunityLineItem. A twin field on the Quote Line will be synced to the matching field on the Opportunity Product when the quote is approved. Learn more ‚Üó
